D.O. (Kyungsoo)

Name: Doh Kyung-soo

Birth Date: January 12, 1993

Birth Place: Nonhyeon-dong, Seoul, South Korea

Height: 172cm - 173cm

Career Overview

Debut: Debuted as a member of EXO under SM Entertainment in 2012 (introduced on Jan 30).

Group: Main vocalist in EXO.

Acting: Made his acting debut in 2014 in the film Cart.

Agency: Left SM Entertainment in late 2023 to join Company Soosoo, and later signed with Company Blitzway.

Key Roles: 100 Days My Prince, Along with the Gods, Swing Kids, My Annoying Brother.

Solo Music: Released his first solo album in 2021.
