Choi Hyun Wook

Name: Choi Hyun-wook

Birth Date: January 30, 2002

Nationality: South Korean

Height: 1.81 m (5′ 11¼″)

Education: Hanlim Multi Art School (Broadcasting & Entertainment Dept)

Agency: Gold Medalist

Years Active: 2019–present

Career Highlights

Background: Formerly a baseball player for 10 years at Gangneung High School, he switched to acting after an elbow injury.

Debut: He began his career in 2019 with the web drama Real:Time:Love.

Breakthrough: Gained recognition through Taxi Driver (2021) and Racket Boys (2021), winning Best New Actor at the 2021 SBS Drama Awards.

Popular Roles: Known for Twenty-Five Twenty-One (2022), Weak Hero Class 1 (2022), and Twinkling Watermelon (2023).
