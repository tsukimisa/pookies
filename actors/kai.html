Kai

Name: Kim Jong-in

Birth Date: January 14, 1994

Birthplace: Suncheon, South Korea

Height: 182 cm (5'11")

Blood Type: A

Education: School of Performing Arts Seoul (SOPA)

Career Highlights

EXO (2011–present): Kai was the first member of EXO-K to be introduced and appeared in 12–13 pre-debut teasers. He is a member of the main group, EXO-K, and the supergroup SuperM.

Soloist (2020–present): He debuted as a solo artist on November 30, 2020, with the EP Kai. He has also released the EPs Peaches and Rover.

Acting: He has appeared in television dramas such as Choco Bank (2016), Andante (2017), Spring Has Come (2018), and The Miracle We Met (2018).
